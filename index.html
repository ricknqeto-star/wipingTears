About Wiping Tears

Wiping Tears is a non-profit organization dedicated to empowering underprivileged girls and women.
We believe that every girl deserves access to education, health, and dignity.

Our Mission

To provide essential resources and opportunities that transform lives and build brighter futures.

Our Core Programs

🩸

Sanitary Products

Distributing free menstrual hygiene kits to girls in need, ensuring they can attend school without worry.

📚

School Fees & Supplies

Sponsoring education costs and providing books, uniforms, and learning materials.

🎓

Educational Classes

Offering workshops on health, skills development, and leadership for women and girls students.
